USING THE SPREADSHEET
================================================================================

For Monthly Tracking (Dashboard KPIs):
1. Enter monthly values in columns B through M
2. Realizado column automatically calculates average
3. % Atingido column shows performance vs. target
4. Color coding shows instant status (Green/Yellow/Red)

For Strategic Planning (Metas 2025-2028):
1. Review multi-year projections
2. Update assumptions (blue text) as needed
3. Monitor growth trajectories and team expansion

For Project Management (Entregáveis):
1. Update status for each deliverable
2. Adjust priorities as needed
3. Use for team coordination and reporting

For Hiring (Estrutura Operacional):
1. Track phases and hiring priorities
2. Monitor salary budgets
3. Update hiring status as roles are filled

For Inventory (Estoque & Fornecedores):
1. Monitor stock levels
2. Review automatically calculated margins
3. Pay attention to REPOR alerts when stock is low
